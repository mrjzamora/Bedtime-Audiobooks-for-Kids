About Us

Our series of audiobooks is designed to help toddlers learn the basics of growing up in a fun and engaging way. Each story follows Lio, who is half Colombian and half Dominican, as he navigates important childhood milestones with the help of his loving family and friends. Lio is a funny and strong-minded little lion who adores his daddy and mommy. He has a passion for soccer, drums, music, and pizza, which makes his adventures both relatable and entertaining for young listeners. Through Lio's playful and heartwarming stories, children will learn essential skills such as brushing their teeth, potty training, and more. Our audiobooks aim to teach valuable lessons about teamwork, patience, and the joy of everyday routines, all while celebrating Lio's unique cultural heritage. Join Lio on his exciting journeys and discover how learning and growing can be an adventure filled with laughter, love, and memorable moments. Each story is carefully crafted to captivate toddlers' imaginations and encourage positive habits in a way that feels natural and enjoyable. We invite you and your little ones to embark on these charming adventures with Lio and make learning a joyful part of growing up.
